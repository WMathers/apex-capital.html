Strategies & Services

How We Generate Alpha

We deploy a suite of systematic trading strategies across crypto derivatives markets — designed for performance in both bull and bear conditions.

01 — Arbitrage
Funding Rate Arbitrage
We exploit persistent pricing inefficiencies between perpetual futures and spot markets. By holding delta-neutral positions, we capture funding payments regardless of market direction — a consistent, low-correlation return stream.
02 — Momentum
Momentum & Trend Following
Our momentum models identify breakout conditions across multiple timeframes and asset pairs. Positions are sized dynamically based on volatility and signal confidence, capturing sustained directional moves with managed downside.
03 — Statistical
Statistical Arbitrage
We monitor correlated asset pairs for mean-reversion opportunities, executing when spreads deviate beyond historical norms. This strategy is largely market-neutral and produces returns independent of broad market direction.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What exchanges do you trade on? ▼
We primarily operate on Binance, OKX, and Bybit — all Tier 1 exchanges with deep liquidity and institutional-grade APIs.
How are fees structured? ▼
We charge a 1.5% annual management fee plus a 15% performance fee on profits above the high-water mark. No hidden fees, ever.
Is my capital locked up? ▼
Managed account clients have a 90-day initial lock-up period. After that, withdrawals can be requested with 14 days notice. Copy trading clients maintain full custody of their own accounts.
Can I monitor my positions in real time? ▼
Yes. Managed account clients receive read-only API access and a live client dashboard to monitor positions, PnL, and drawdown at any time.
What is the minimum investment? ▼
$25,000 for managed accounts and $5,000 for copy trading. Strategy consulting is available regardless of account size — reach out for custom pricing.
Do you provide tax documents? ▼
We provide detailed trade logs and monthly performance summaries that can be shared with your accountant or tax professional. We recommend consulting a CPA familiar with digital asset taxation.
